Description
AVA Softphone is the lightweight mobile companion for your company phone system. It auto-syncs with your PBX so you always see live call history, call recordings with AI transcripts, queue activity, voicemail and a domain-wide stats dashboard.

Highlights:
• Live company call history with one-tap callback.
• Domain dashboard: total calls, answered, missed, top extensions, 7-day trend.
• Call queues (read-only): waiting count, agents online, SLA, average wait.
• Call recordings with AI transcription, sentiment and summary.
• Voicemail inbox with transcriptions.
• AVA AI assistant — ask anything about your phone system.
• Push notifications for incoming calls, missed calls and voicemails.
• Built for regular extension users — no admin clutter.

Privacy & security:
• All telephony data is scoped by your organization and extension.
• Recordings are streamed through short-lived signed URLs — no PBX secrets on device.
• You can delete your account directly from the app (Settings → More → Delete my account).
