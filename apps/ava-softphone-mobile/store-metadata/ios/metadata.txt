Description
Lemtel AI Phone brings your business communications into one elegant mobile app. Make and receive calls, manage SMS conversations, listen to voicemail, review call recordings, read transcripts, and use AVA AI to summarize calls, create follow-up actions, generate auto-attendant greetings, and connect voice agents.

Highlights:
• Premium dialer with HD calling on Wi-Fi and mobile data.
• SMS & MMS inbox with templates and AVA reply assist.
• Voicemail with transcription, sentiment, and priority.
• Call recordings with waveform, transcripts, and AI summaries.
• AVA AI workspace: summaries, action items, greeting studio, voice agents.
• Admin tools for IVRs, queues, ring groups, and number assignment.

Access is controlled by your organization's permissions. All sensitive telephony operations are handled securely through AVA backend services — no PBX or SIP secrets are stored on the device.
